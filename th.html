Founding Team

Founded in 2024, LUNA is a prodromically Hispanic team that strives to spread its STEM fields throughout our Hispanic culture. Our team name, “Luna” comes from the Spanish word for moon. As a team, we feel like this helps define not only our identity, but everyone's since we work to inspire and support all communities, regarding background.

Current Progress

Through LUNA’s 2025-2026 season, we were able to consistently adapt and develop solutions to our teams challenges we found along the way. With this process, we were able to strengthen our problem solving abilities and representing our teams culture in a proffesional way. As a team, we continue to push our boundaries through our technical and non tecnial side. Through lunas meets, we aim to showcase our teams pride and achivembts with the help of mentors.
