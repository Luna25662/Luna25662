Founded in 2024, LUNA is a prodromically Hispanic team that strives to spread its STEM fields throughout our Hispanic culture. Our team name, “Luna” comes from the Spanish word for moon. As a team, we feel like this helps define not only our identity, but everyone's since we work to inspire and support all communities, regarding background.

Had been formed in 2024, LUNA continues to strive and connect with many communities and teams while showing gracious professionalism and representation. Through our meets and kickoffs, we aim to show respect throughout all FTC robotic teams regardless of identity while also bringing a safe space for teams and members. As the seasons continues, LUNA continues to adapt and find solutions to problems we find thought the way. And as we aim to continue this, were able to help represent our identity while respecting those around ours. As we bring a safe space for teams, we help bring creativity into our community by valuing FIRST’s main values. We aim to help push the boundaries of ourselves and our culture while also helping other communities
